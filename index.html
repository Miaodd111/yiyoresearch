About Us

YiYo was established in Hong Kong in 2021 and is a global market research company. We focus on the overseas track and are committed to providing global one-stop research services for gaming and Internet companies going overseas to empower companies with their overseas strategies.

  • Our research resources cover the world's mainstream overseas markets, with more than 300 million user resources around the world, including Asia-Pacific, Europe, America, Latin America, the Middle East and other regions. No matter which market our clients wish to enter, we can provide professional and customized research services based on their needs.
  • Our team members all have more than 10 years of market research experience. The core members have experience in building an overseas research process from 0 to 1 and have executed a large number of overseas research projects.
  • The team focuses on the gaming and Internet fields and has user research experience in over a hundred games. The projects include MOBA, SLG, SOC, MMO, ARPG, action, shooting, sandbox, placement, card, two-dimensional, female-oriented and other mainstream categories. Covering various platforms such as PC/Steam, consoles, and mobile phones.
